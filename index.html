Big Features, Small Size

Triple-slot GPU support

Supporting graphics cards of up to 330mm (inc power connector) in length and 60mm of width (triple slot)

360 Degree Accessibility

Removeable panels enable easy access to the front, back and sides of the case for easy installation

Out-of-the-box Custom Cooling Support

Support for custom loop and AIO radiators of up to 280mm on the side and 240mm on the bottom

High Quality Materials

Thick, powder coated SGCC steel keeps the fit and finish of the chassis clean and durable even with harsh traveling conditions.

Unrestricted Cooling

The NR200 can house up to 7 fans in total for every cooling configuration imaginable. Five panels are flanked with ventilation for unrestricted airflow.

Easy, Tool-Free Access

All external panels and top mounted fans are secured with pins for easy removal, facilitating quick acces to the internals without the use of tools.
